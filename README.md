# Electric Peak Engineering (EPE)

EPE is a Montana-based firm specializing in the design and integration of Distributed Energy Resources (DER). We bridge the gap between rigorous mechanical engineering and innovative software solutions to create modern energy systems.

## 🛠 Strategic Services
- **Feasibility Studies:** Technical, economic, and environmental viability assessments for onsite power.
- **System Design:** Detailed engineering of integrated DER systems (Solar PV, BESS, CHP, and Microgrids).
- **Owner's Engineer:** Independent oversight, vendor negotiation, and project execution.
- **Interconnection & Permitting:** Navigating complex regulatory landscapes and utility standards.
- **Measurement & Verification:** Performance tracking and optimization of deployed DER.
  
## ⚙️ Technical Expertise
- **Engineering Core:** Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, and Heat Transfer.
- **Modeling Suite:** NREL’s SAM/PySAM, REopt, EnergyPlus, and Autodesk.
- **Numerical Methods:** High-fidelity simulation using specialized Python engineering libraries.
- **Infrastructure:** Scalable cloud architecture, Dockerized environments, and secure data management.

## 🚀 The Electric Peak Platform
We are currently developing a modular workbench to streamline DER project lifecycles — from initial qualification to performance verification.
